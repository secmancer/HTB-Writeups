# What is a section?
- A **section** is the smallest learning unit in HTB Academy.
- Each section focuses on a specific subtopic within a module.
- Sections are designed to be completed in a single sitting.
- Example: In the _Intro to Academy_ module, the _Sections_ section explains what sections are.


# Types of Sections
- Sections are divided into two types: **Theory** and **Interactive**.
- **Theory Sections**:
    - Contain no practical exercises.
    - Provide foundational knowledge needed for later interactive content.
    - Establish the core concepts of the module.
- **Interactive Sections**:
    - Represent the majority of HTB Academy sections.
    - Include practical examples, demos, and hands-on learning.
    - Encourage learners to re-apply concepts to reinforce understanding.


# My Workstation
- Each module provides a personal **workstation (PwnBox)** for hands-on practice.
- The workstation is a **Linux system (Parrot OS)** preloaded with learning and security tools.
- It allows you to:
    - Reproduce examples from the Academy
    - Complete exercises and Skills Assessments
- You can start your workstation by clicking **Start Instance** at:
    - The end of any interactive section
    - The **My Workstation** box on the right side of the screen

# Completing a Section
- Click **Mark Complete and Next** to finish a section.
- If the section includes **questions**, you must answer them before it can be marked complete.
- You can skip or return to sections later by:
    - Clicking **Next**, or
    - Selecting a section directly from the **Table of Contents**.
