- HTB Academy organizes content into structured learning blocks.
- The largest unit is a **path**, composed of multiple **modules**.
- Each **module** covers a specific topic and is divided into **sections**.
- Certain **paths** are tied to certifications, awarded after completing the path and its exam.

![[Pasted image 20251016213510.png]]

- Paths may appear extensive or overwhelming at first.
- Dividing them into modules and sections makes the material easier to absorb.
- Learners can progress gradually, one section at a time.
- This step-by-step approach leads to achieving larger goals, such as earning a certificate.

![[Pasted image 20251016213555.png]]

- The learning process encourages steady, step-by-step progress.
- Constant testing and challenges reinforce understanding.
- This approach transforms learned concepts into practical, retained skills.
