# Pick a Path
- Students are encouraged to **follow a specific path** for structured learning.
- **Beginners:**
    - Start with the **Junior Cybersecurity Analyst Path** to earn the **HTB Certified Junior Cybersecurity Associate** certification.
    - Requires **no prior experience** and prepares you for intermediate certifications.
- **Intermediate learners:**
    - Choose an **intermediate job-role path** based on your career interests.
- **Advanced learners:**
    - Enroll in **advanced job-role paths**, such as:
        - **Senior Web Penetration Tester** → _HTB Certified Web Exploitation Expert_
        - **Active Directory Penetration Tester**
- HTB Academy regularly adds new **modules**, **paths**, and **certifications** — stay updated for upcoming content.
- **Good luck on your learning journey!**
