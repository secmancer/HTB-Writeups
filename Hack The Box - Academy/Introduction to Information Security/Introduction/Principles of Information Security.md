# Core Principles
- Confidentiality
	- Accessible only to authorized users
	- Protects against unauthorized disclosure
	- Encryption, access controls
- Integrity
	- Ensure accuracy/data completeness
	- Protection against unauthorized modification
	- Hashing, digital signatures
- Availability
	- Authorized users can access when needed
	- Protection against disruption
	- Redundancy, disaster recovery planning
- Non-repudiation
	- Cannot deny authenticity of a signature on a document/sent message
	- E-commerce  / legal context focus
	- Digital signatures, audit logs
- Authentication
	- Verifies the identity of user/process/device
	- Only authorized entities are allowed in
	- Passwords, bio-metrics, MFA
- Privacy
	- Proper handling of sensitive personal information
	- Data protection compliance
	- Data minimization, consent management
